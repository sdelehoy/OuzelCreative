Sustainability at Ouzel Creative

We inspire global stewardship by informing eco-conscious decisions with choices for digital and print media that reduce environmental impact because present and future people, flora and fauna deserve their place in a thriving ecosystem.

We recognize that we have an impact on the environment. Our commitment is to minimize that impact. To be fully transparent, we acknowledge that we are not perfect in this endeavor. Perfection is not the goal. However, progress, and reaching towards perfection is our goal.

We believe that running a business should not come at the expense of people and the planet.

We believe that being sustainable doesn't come at the expense of good design. A project can implement earth-friendly choices and have a great end product.

Our Why

Being Good Stewards. We acknowledge that we share this planet with other creatures (such as the Ouzel), plants and people. Our environment is something that is shared and relied upon for survival by all. We aim to be good stewards of it so that others may continue to benefit and thrive.

Necessary for Survival and Thriving. Nature deserves to be protected as a crucial element to health and survival. Clean water, soil and air are necessary for producing food for nourishing and sustaining life.

Nature has been scientifically proven to be therapeutic for humans both mentally and physically. (A quick search on the benefits of nature will bring up numerous articles and research on the subject. If you would like to read more here are a couple of books we have read: The Nature Fix and Forest Bathing.)

Source of Inspiration. We enjoy hiking, backpacking and bird watching. Exploring wild places and soaking in the beauty that surrounds us is an inspiration for our creativity. Creatures and plants continually influence our design choices and rejuvenate our ability to create beauty in our work. Birds have been vanishing at an alarming rate mostly due to human activities. We can't imagine a world without birdsong. We want to see natural sites and sounds protected so that they can continue to be enjoyed and explored by future generations.

Our How

Sustainable Practices for Our Business

Tracking Progress

  • We participate in the Colorado Green Business Network and work with them to set goals and meet requirements. We have been recognized by them as achieving a Gold level of business sustainability.
  • We measure the sustainability of our website and make incremental improvements.

Offsetting

  • We donate 1% of payments received via credit card to carbon capture through Stripe, our payment processing service provider.

Office Supplies

  • We are mindful of sustainability when making purchases for our office and give preference to recycled, non-toxic and locally sourced products as much as possible.
  • We recycle or responsibly dispose of electronics, printer cartridges, batteries, paper and plastic wrap as much as possible.
  • We us non-toxic cleaning agents when possible.

Energy Efficiency

  • We use LED lights.
  • We turn off lights and unplug office equipment when not in use.
  • We use efficiency settings on our computers and put them into sleep mode or shutdown during off hours.
  • We have regular maintenance performed on our HVAC and use a programmable thermostat to power down its use during unoccupied times.
  • We use a space heater when needed in our home office and turn down the heat in the rest of the house during office hours.
  • We have added insulation to the attic and hot water piping to reduce heat loss.
  • We have installed interior window inserts to insulate our windows.
  • We use green web hosting that offsets energy consumption and an email marketing platform that uses servers powered by renewable energy.

Water Conservation

  • We inspect for water leaks.
  • We plant low-water and native plants in landscaping to conserve water.

Business Travel

  • We work from a home office and use virtual communication as an alternative to regular travel to save on gas consumption.
  • When air travel is unavoidable, we are committed to prioritizing direct flights and avoid first class whenever possible.
  • We have begun a process for tracking our carbon footprint in business travel.

Sustainable Practices for Our Clients

  • We use print services that implement sustainable practices as much as possible. These print services offer recycled and FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) certified paper options. We work with a local screen printer that can print using water-based ink on 100% cotton.
  • We offer options for green hosting providers for websites and CRM platforms with sustainability policies.
  • We strive to implement sustainable digital media and web design practices that include but not limited to: accessibility, decreased image file size, better SEO that allows people to find websites faster, good UX and clearly written content allowing customers to get what they need quickly.

Sustainable Practices for Our Community

  • We seek out opportunities to participate in community projects that support environmental stewardship (e.g. community cleanup).
  • We have begun to educate the community on digital sustainability through our website, social media and partners.

Our Goals

  • We are making plans to organize an annual river cleanup in our area.
  • We are working towards driving a fully electric vehicle by 2027.
  • We aim to make a 25% reduction in our office's energy consumption by 2025.
